Why parallel?
Each critic runs simultaneously — 3x faster than serial.
The logic critic uses deepseek-reasoner and takes ~10s. Running sequentially would mean 10s + 4s + 4s = 18s total. In parallel, total time equals the slowest critic alone (~10s). Thread pool overhead is <5ms.
◆
Why different models?
Independent blind spots — one model's weakness is another's strength.
deepseek-chat is fast and great at factual recall. deepseek-reasoner is slower but excels at chain-of-thought logic. Using both means logical contradictions get caught even when the fast model misses them.
✓
Why an adjudicator?
Critics disagree 23% of the time — someone makes the final call.
The completeness critic often flags things the factual critic rates as fine. The adjudicator resolves these by checking if the flagged issue is actually a gap or just a concise answer. It also dismisses false positives — confirmed issues only make the final verdict.
